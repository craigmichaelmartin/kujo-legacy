Our Story
Tired of gripless worn out tennis shoes and the lack of better options, our founder decided to foster a brand, a company, a culture that not only offered a solution, but engaged homeowners that find pride and joy in their yards. It was time for yard gear that truly captured the terrain of the yard without skimping on the style.
Waterproof Latex
The Kujo, our namesake and CEO’s cousin, was a guy who always had to be outside. As kids, he shared the love for being in the yard by getting those Langton cousins outside, despite their longings for his video games. Shawn still hears The Kujo’s voice reminding him, “let’s go outside!”
Our Culture
We love our yards! Our pride is in freshly cut grass and our joy is enjoying it with a cold one because nothing beats opening up the backdoor to your own paradise. Soak up the sun, work up a sweat, host a blow out, show off your flowerbeds... as long as you’re spending time outside, you’re doing it right. Meanwhile, we’ll make sure you’re clothed in nothing but the best in premium yardwear.
